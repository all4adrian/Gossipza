Objectives

Gossipza is a website for entertainment and learning. It shares trending topics about pizzas, trending topics, and pizza-related content. It is made for internet users and pizza lovers who like fun and useful information. The website gets content from researched sources. It has a blog with pizza history, fun facts, and top pizza rankings based on popularity. It also has a pizza maze game for fun and learning.

Top 3 favored flavor pizzas

Pepperoni Pizza - A pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and slices of spicy pepperoni. It’s crispy, cheesy, and full of flavor.

Hawaiian Pizza - This pizza has tomato sauce, cheese, ham, and pineapple. The mix of sweet pineapple and salty ham makes it stand out

Ham and Cheese Pizza - A simple pizza with tomato sauce, melted cheese, and ham It’s easy and delicious

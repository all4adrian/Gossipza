5 Fun Facts about pizza's

1. Hawaiian Pizza Origin: The Hawaiian pizza was invented by a Greek man, Sam Panopoulos in Canada in 1962. He added pineapple to attract more customers.

2. Pizza Theorem: There's a mathematical theorem called the "Pizza Theorem" which deals with the partitioning of a disk and the equality of areas.

3. World's Largest Pizza: The largest circular pizza was made in South Africa in 1990, measuring 122 feet in diameter and weighing 26,883 pounds.

4. Pizza Consumption: Americans consume about 350 slices of pizza per second, resulting in around 3 billion pizzas sold annually.

5. Unique Toppings: In Sweden, banana is a common pizza topping, often paired with curry powder for a spicy twist.
